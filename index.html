About Me

Music is something that everyone should fall in love with. Sometimes music can be purely fun, energetic, and meaningless. Sometimes, it can be created with such a fine tuned precision, that you can't help but relate to. Those songs almost become a form of therapy and can help you through amazing times, or even the worst. That's why I came up with the name 'Empty Advice.' I craft music that you can relate to. If you're a fan of chill, lo-fi, jazz music: I think you'll love my sound.
